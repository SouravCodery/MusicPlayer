Armaan Malik (born 22 July 1995) is an Indian singer, songwriter and an actor. He is known for his singing in Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Tamil, Bengali, Gujarati & Marathi. A finalist on Sa Re Ga Ma Pa L'il Champs (2006), Armaan advanced to eighth place with public votes. He is the brother of composer Amaal Mallik. Previously represented by Universal Music India, he is now signed on by T-Series. He has also signed with Arista records.
